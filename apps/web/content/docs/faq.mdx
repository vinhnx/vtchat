# Frequently Asked Questions

Here are answers to the most commonly asked questions about our platform.

## General Questions

### What is VT?
VT is an AI-powered chat platform that provides access to multiple AI models and advanced conversation capabilities. We focus on privacy, performance, and user experience.

### Is VT free to use?
Yes, we offer a free tier with access to basic AI models and features. We also provide VT+ premium subscriptions for advanced capabilities and higher usage limits.

### What AI models are available?
We provide access to various AI models including:
- OpenAI GPT models
- Anthropic Claude models
- Google Gemini models
- Other specialized models for specific use cases

## Account & Privacy

### How do I create an account?
Simply visit our platform and sign up with your email address or use social login options. Account creation is quick and straightforward.

### Is my conversation data private?
Yes, your privacy is our top priority. We don't use your conversations to train AI models, and all data is encrypted and securely stored.

### Can I delete my conversation history?
Absolutely! You can delete individual conversations or your entire chat history at any time from your account settings.

### Do you share data with third parties?
We only share data with AI model providers as necessary to provide the service. We never sell or share your personal conversations with other parties.

## Features & Usage

### What's the difference between chat modes?
- **Standard Chat**: Regular AI conversation
- **Deep Research**: In-depth analysis with multiple sources
- **Pro Search**: Real-time web search integration

### Can I upload files?
Yes, you can upload documents, images, and data files for AI analysis. Supported formats include PDF, DOC, JPG, PNG, CSV, and more.

### What are the usage limits?
Free users have daily limits on messages and certain features. VT+ subscribers get significantly higher limits and priority access.

### How do I choose the right AI model?
Different models excel at different tasks:
- Use general models for everyday conversation
- Choose specialized models for coding, writing, or analysis
- Select multimodal models when working with images or documents

## Technical Questions

### Is there an API available?
Yes, we offer API access for developers. Contact us for API documentation and access credentials.

### Can I use VT on mobile?
Yes, our platform is fully responsive and works great on mobile devices. We also offer a progressive web app (PWA) experience.

### What browsers are supported?
We support all modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. For the best experience, we recommend using the latest version of your preferred browser.

## Billing & Subscriptions

### How does VT+ billing work?
VT+ is billed monthly or annually. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel at any time from your account settings.

### What payment methods do you accept?
We accept major credit cards, PayPal, and other payment methods depending on your region.

### Can I get a refund?
We offer refunds within the first 30 days of subscription for any reason. Contact our support team for assistance.

- Check this FAQ first

- Contact our support team via email
- Join our community forums

### Is there premium support?
VT+ subscribers get priority support with faster response times and dedicated assistance.
